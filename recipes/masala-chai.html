Masala Chai

This is all time favorite and Everyone's Pasandida Morning and Evening Beverage.

Many People's day not even start with masala-chai.

#Ingrediants:

  • Chai Patti
  • Ellaichi(for best fragrance)
  • Clove
  • Chai Masala, You can buy from any local Shop

# Steps to make My favorite Dish

  1. Put water and add some Milk in beaker
  2. After it get's warm, add Chai patti in it
  3. Add clove, Ellaichi, and Chai Masala in it
  4. Later add required amount of Sugar
  5. Boil for 2 to 3 Minutes
  6. Enjoy Your masala-chai
